Data Visualisation in PowerBI

With four other students, we set out to better understand wealth, education, and other social dimensions by visualizing OECD data in PowerBI.

Focusing on the analysis of well-being and economic indicators across OECD countries, this PowerBI report aims to shed interactive light on the macroeconomic factors that member states faced in 2017, when Russia was still a member of the organization. This project uses Power BI to analyze a dataset provided by the OECD, which covers a wide range of topics related to societal well-being and economic performance across its member countries. The original dataset includes 34 topics covering various aspects of well-being, such as housing quality, income, health, employment, and work-life balance, among others. Our goal is to use Power BI's dynamic reporting and interactive visualization capabilities to present a comprehensive analysis of these indicators.
