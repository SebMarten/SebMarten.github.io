Humanizing AI

'Accenture AI Leaders Podcast' just released their newest podcast.

I find the topic of responsible AI practices discussed in the "Accenture AI Leaders Podcast" episode featuring Sanjay Podder and Dr. Rana el Kaliouby particularly intriguing due to its direct relevance to the field of data and business analytics. In today's fast-paced technological world, the integration of AI within business processes is becoming increasingly prevalent. This episode sheds light on an essential aspect often overlooked: the ethical implications and the importance of human and emotional considerations in the development and deployment of AI technologies. By emphasizing a harmonious relationship between technology and humanity, the discussion underscores the necessity for analytics professionals to incorporate ethical considerations into their data-driven strategies. This approach not only enhances the trustworthiness and acceptance of AI solutions across various stakeholders but also ensures that technological advancements contribute positively to society.

Introduction to Tableau

Mo Chen showcases how to use Tableau effectively.

After hearing much about Tableau's prowess in data visualization, I decided to expand my business analytics skill set by learning how to use it. Mo Chen's "Tableau portfolio project" video was a perfect starting point. I dove into the process of building an interactive dashboard, importing data, and creating diverse visualizations, like line charts that elucidate sales trends, and histograms that reveal price distributions. The inclusion of a calendar widget as a filter particularly intrigued me—it offered a dynamic way to sift through time-based data. This capability is indispensable for making strategic business decisions, as it allows for real-time analysis and forecasting. By learning to construct and publish a dashboard, I gained the ability to present data in an engaging, informative manner. This skill is not just an addition to my portfolio; it's an essential component of modern business analytics that turns data into dialogue.

October 15th, 2023

Democtatisation of AI

This TED Talk with Andrew Ng offers stunning insights.

On my blog, I often talk about AI. Although this video is a bit older, Andrew Ng's TED Talk on democratizing access to artificial intelligence was deeply inspiring, especially the concept of making AI accessible to businesses of all sizes, not just large tech companies. Ng's vision of enabling even local pizza shops to use AI to predict the best-selling flavors on any given day underscores a crucial shift towards leveraging technology to enhance decision-making, productivity, and profitability across the board. This approach is paramount for Business Analytics because it underscores the potential of AI to transform decision-making processes, making them more data-driven and efficient. By democratizing AI, businesses can harness the power of their data to optimize operations, forecast trends, and tailor their offerings to meet customer demand more accurately. This not only opens up new avenues for innovation and competition but also contributes to a more dynamic and resilient economy.
